Dial-In Checklist

If brew tastes sour

Grind finer, raise water temp by 1-2C, and extend contact time slightly.

If brew tastes bitter

Grind coarser, reduce temperature, and shorten total extraction time.

If body feels thin

Increase dose 1-2g or reduce final yield to strengthen texture and sweetness.
